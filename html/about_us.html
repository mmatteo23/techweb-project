About us...

Who are we?

The development team is composed of four graduate students at Padova University: Matteo Casonato, Matteo Midena, Davide Milan and Danilo StojkoviÄ‡. The site you're currently on was made as a project for the Web Technologies course.

What is it about?

Our main goal is to present the latest news concerning the world of electronic sports, videogames played competitevely by professional players. This side of videogames is something we're pretty passionate about so we decided to cover it on this website.

What are our core values?

The main principle and the concept that inspired the name is the five Ws (Penta is greek for five); they each represent one question in the English language:

  • Who
  • What
  • When
  • Where
  • Why

These are the questions each one of our editors needs to answer while writing an article.

Who can write for us?

Just about anyone, all you need to do is Sign up and create an account on the website, then you can send us a "become-a-writer" request via the form at the bottom of this page which we will review.

We already have dozens of gamers editing the site but we could always benefit from YOUR help.

How to reach us

We are always available at our company e-mail address: support@pentanews.com where you can ask any question about the site, suggest changes or apply to become an admin and write articles for us!

We truly think your feedback is a key element to making Penta News an even better website.

Want to become a journalist for us?

Send us a request with a brief explanation of your background history as a journalist!
