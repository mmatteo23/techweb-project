About us...

Who are we?

The development team is composed of four graduate students at Padova University: Matteo Casonato, Matteo Midena, Davide Milan and Danilo Stojković. The site you're currently on was made as a project for the Web Technology course.

What is it about?

Our main goal is to present information and the latest news concerning the world of electronic sports, which are basically multiplayer games played competitevely by usually professional players. This side of videogames is something we're pretty passionate about so we decided to cover it on this site.

What are our core values?

The main principle and the concept that inspired the name is the five Ws (Penta is greek for five); they each represent one question in the English language:

  • Who
  • What
  • When
  • Where
  • Why

These are the questions each one of our editors needs to answer while writing an article.

Who can write for us?

Just about anyone, all you need to do is Sign up and get a profile on the site, then you can send a "become-a-writer" request which we will review.

We already have dozens of gamers editing the site but we could always benefit from YOUR help.

How to reach us

We are always available on our company e-mail address: support@penta-news.com where you can ask any question about the site or suggest changes.

We truly think your feedback is a key element to making Penta News and even better site.
